# VPN (Virtual Private Network)

- VPN creates an **encrypted tunnel** between your device and VPN server.
- Protects data from eavesdropping on public networks.
- Hides your IP → websites see VPN server’s IP, not yours.

## Demo
- Connect to a VPN (e.g., NordVPN, ProtonVPN).
- Check IP before and after connection (`whatismyipaddress.com`).

## Note
- May reduce speed slightly.
- Useful for privacy or accessing region-restricted content.
